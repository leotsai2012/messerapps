Messer is the largest privately managed industrial gases specialist and is active in more than 30 countries in Europe and Asia, as well as Peru, Algeria and New Zealand, with a total of over 60 operating companies. Its international activities are managed from Frankfurt am Main, whilst management of core technical functions – logistics, engineering, production and applications engineering – is undertaken from Krefeld.
